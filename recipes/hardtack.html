Ramen

Description

Hardtack is used by me as an effeicent way of getting enough nutrients to survive the night

Survial soup is made up of easily sourced ingredients and also is readily made

ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix the flour, water and salt in a bowl
  2. Spread, flatten and cut dough
  3. place in oven and allow to bake
  4. Remove from oven and allow to cool
  5. Enjoy!... or not....
