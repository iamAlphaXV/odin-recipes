Survival Soup

Description

Survial soup is used by me as an effeicent way of getting enough nutrients to survive the night

Survial soup is made up of easily sourced ingredients and also is readily made

ingredients

  • Milk
  • Crix
  • Corn Flakes

Steps

  1. Pour milk into bowl
  2. Crush crix and pour into bowl
  3. Pour corn flakes into bowl
  4. Mix thouroughly
  5. Enjoy!... or not....
